About Us

The Methodist Church Ghana, an offshoot of the Wesleyan tradition, is committed to spreading the Gospel, fostering discipleship, and transforming lives. Guided by the teachings of John Wesley, we aim to build a Christ-centered community that promotes love, faith, and service in all aspects of life.
